This tool estimates the likelihood of heterotopic ossification formation after acetabular fracture fixation through a posterior approach. It is derived from a retrospective multicenter cohort of 1,288 adult patients treated without HO prophylaxis.

Clinical Disclaimer

This calculator is intended to support, not replace, clinical judgment. Predicted probabilities are derived from a retrospective two-center cohort and have not yet been externally validated in an independent prospective dataset. Risk estimates should be interpreted in the context of individual patient circumstances, surgeon experience, and institutional resources. Race is included as a predictor variable based on its independent statistical association in the derivation cohort; its biological basis requires further investigation. This tool does not constitute medical advice and should not be used as the sole basis for clinical decision-making.
