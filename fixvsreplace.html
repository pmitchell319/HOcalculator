Decision Support

Estimates fixation failure probability and replacement complication risk across femoral neck, femoral head, and acetabular fractures to support perioperative decision-making.

This tool provides individualized risk estimates to support the fix vs replace decision in periarticular hip fractures. Fixation failure outputs reflect preliminary models under development; replacement risk estimates are derived from large database studies. External validation is ongoing. This tool is intended to support, not replace, clinical judgment.

Clinical disclaimer

This tool is in development and has not yet been externally validated. Fixation failure probabilities are preliminary estimates based on published literature and existing datasets; validated models are in development. Replacement risk estimates are derived from large database studies and reflect population-level complication rates — fracture-specific variables including pattern, bone loss, and soft tissue injury were not incorporated and may substantially alter individual risk. This tool is intended to support, not replace, clinical judgment and should not be used as the sole basis for treatment decisions.
